Why Ethical Hackers Are Important

Ethical hackers are important because they help protect computer systems and networks by finding security weaknesses before real criminals can exploit them. They use the same skills as malicious hackers, but their goal is to improve safety, not cause harm. By testing and identifying vulnerabilities, ethical hackers help organizations fix problems and prevent data breaches or cyberattacks.
